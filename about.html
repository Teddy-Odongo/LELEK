Lelek picnics

Lelek Picnics was started on 13 March 2021. Our founder was planning for a birthday picnic for her husband and the existing companies that offered picnic services were too expensive for her and she thought about creating beautiful experiences for other people but on a budget. She started the business with her husband and now the company consists of an amazing team that is very professional and creative delivering curated unforgettable experiences for all our clients.
