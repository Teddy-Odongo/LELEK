FAQs?

1. HOW DO I BOOK AN EXPERIENCE?

You can fill out our booking request form, call/text or email us. We will contact you for details on how to customize your picnic.

2.DO YOU HAVE SUGGESTED PICNIC LOCATIONS?

We are located in Nairobi and do setups across the major towns in Kenya.

3.HOW LONG DOES AN EXPERIENCE LAST?

You get to enjoy a full day experience. We can set up as early as you would like but set down is by 7 p.m. as this is the time most gardens close. However, for our Bubble tent and Cinema experience we have identified a location that allows overnight bookings.

4.HOW FAR IN ADVANCE DO I BOOK?

We recommend booking at soon as you identify the date of your event. This is because we confirm booking on a first paid basis. Depending on availability we may be able to accommodate last minute bookings.

5.DO THE PICNICS COME WITH FOOD?

We work with amazing catering partners that provide bitings and food for your picnic.

6.HOW MANY GUESTS CAN I HAVE?

We can accommodate up to 40 guests.

7.WHAT IF IT RAINS?

You can plan a pretty picnic but you can’t predict the weather! We recommend having a backup picnic location (preferably indoors) where we can continue the event as planned. We cannot refund payments due to weather cancellations but we can reschedule according to availability.

8.DO I NEED TO PAY A DEPOSIT?

We require at least 50% deposit to hold your event date.
